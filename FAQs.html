Frequently Asked Questions


• The Mzamomtsha School uniform has been designed to be affordable and versatile. Many of the items can be worn outside of school as well.
• You can buy school uniform from the school. Please visit the school for information on uniform and prices.

• Mzamomtsha Schools does not offer holiday care over term break or public holidays. Please review the instructional calendar to plan care for your child accordingly.

• There are many different positions available at Mzamomtsha School. To view these positions and requirements to apply please visit our website.

• Mzamomtsha School offers a personalized learning model of education. This model ensures that each scholar receives the academic attention that they require as an individual through one-on-one and small group learning during the school day.
• In the Grade R classes we have no more than 25 scholars, and in Grade 1 and upwards we have no more than 32 scholars however each of these scholars are receiving the academic attention that they require. Please read more about our Learning Model on our website.

• Mzamomtsha school do offer extra-mural activities. Kindly visit our website, under Events page for more information.

• Mzamomtsha school will host Open Days at different points in the year. Please keep an eye on our Events page to view these events.

• Staff can be contacted via phone calls, e-mails or SMSs. Credentials will be provided according to your child's classes. An appointment can be made via the school office.

• Sorry, the school does not provide transportation yet, but we are working towards buying a bus or taxis for learners in far distances.

• All Teaching Assistants are first aid trained and can offer reassurance and first aid to pupils who are injured.
• If injury is significant, we would inform parents and appropriate first aid would be given and of course any serious injuries occurrence would resort to immediate medical service involvement.
